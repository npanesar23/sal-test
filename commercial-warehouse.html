Commercial & Industrial Warehouse

Sals Power Washing supports warehouses, plants, and industrial sites across Cornwall, SD&G, and Ottawa with washing, degreasing, and confined-space programs, coordinated around your safety rules and production schedule.

Interior & exterior building washing

Exterior and interior building and property cleaning, graffiti removal, gas station canopies, dumpster pads, and high-reach work where certified, using appropriate pressure, hot water, and detergents for the substrate.

Floor cleaning & concrete

Warehouse, garage, and concrete floor degreasing and resealing; deep cleaning for high-traffic aisles; ice thaw and steaming where needed, built around your downtime windows.

Facility services & confined spaces

Confined space cleaning for vessels, entries, and tight industrial areas: trained specialists, safe equipment, and methods aimed at removing sludge, mud, sand, and debris. Single visits or ongoing programs for food plants, oil & gas support sites, and general industry across Eastern Ontario.

Bundled facility support: drain jetting, sanitizing, food processing plant work to scope, and coordination with your maintenance calendar.
