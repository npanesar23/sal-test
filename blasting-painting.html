Blasting & Painting

Sals Power Washing offers wet blasting (sand and water mix) and related methods for rust and paint removal, equipment refurbishment, and tough industrial substrates, with service available across Ontario, Canada, with a particular focus on Cornwall, SD&G, and Ottawa.

Mobile Sandblasting

On-site abrasive blasting for structural steel, equipment, and concrete with containment and recovery appropriate to your site.

Wetblasting

Slurry and wet abrasive options that reduce dust while removing coatings and profiling metal and concrete, aligned with repaint or coating schedules.

Hydroblasting

Ultra-high-pressure water for coating removal and surface prep where water is the right tool, including work paired with drain jetting and line flushing to 6" where specified.

Tank Coating

Surface prep and lining work for storage tanks and process vessels, aligned with coating manufacturer requirements.

Truck & Equipment Painting

Blast/prep and paint for commercial trucks, heavy equipment, and rolling stock with finishes suited to service conditions.

Commercial Exterior & Interior Painting

Full-service commercial painting for building exteriors and interiors, including surface preparation, priming, and finish coats suited to industrial and commercial environments. We coordinate with your schedule and safety requirements across Eastern Ontario.
