About Hydro Tek

Established in 1996, Hydro Tek has grown into Ontario’s trusted partner for industrial surface preparation. For more than 28 years, we’ve helped contractors and owners prep concrete to spec—safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

Our fleet includes shot blasters, water blasters, and large-format grinders operated by trained crews. When your project demands precision and throughput, we bring the right machine and the right team.
