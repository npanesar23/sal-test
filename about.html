Equipment & methods

We invest in state-of-the-art commercial equipment, including hot-water capability for demanding jobs. For homes we prioritize soft washing; higher pressure is reserved for concrete, brickwork, and other hard surfaces where it belongs.

Cleaning agents matter: pressure alone only blasts surface dirt. Proper detergents treat mold, moss, and organic growth at the source so it doesn’t return right away. Our team selects products with guidance from our commercial chemical partners, with an emphasis on safety for people, pets, and landscaping when used as directed.

Frequently asked questions

Will high-pressure water damage my property?

“Pressure washing” covers many services. At Sals we soft wash your home and use pressure washing for concrete, brickwork, and other hard surfaces. Every job still uses appropriate cleaning agents to address the root cause. High pressure alone is not enough to stop mold and moss from coming back quickly.

Do you have insurance?

Yes. Our company carries $2,000,000 liability coverage so you have peace of mind while we work on your home or business. We have years of experience and strong client retention across residential and commercial work.

Are your chemicals harmful?

Our equipment is built for tough cleaning, but that doesn’t mean your family, staff, or landscaping have to suffer. We work with a commercial chemical supplier so our solutions and methods are chosen to be safe for people and the environment when applied correctly.
