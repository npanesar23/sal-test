Power washing services

Sals Power Washing serves Cornwall, the Stormont, Dundas and Glengarry (SD&G) Region, and Ottawa with professional exterior cleaning. We combine hot-water-capable commercial equipment with the right detergents and techniques for each surface.

Residential services (not limited to)

  • House washing with soft-wash methods
  • Roof cleaning
  • Deck and pool areas
  • Driveways: concrete, interlock, brick, or asphalt
  • Interlocking brick polymeric re-sand and sealing
  • Fences

Commercial services (not limited to)

  • Building and property cleaning
  • Dumpster pad and dumpster sanitizing
  • Fleet washing and heavy equipment
  • Acid wash (where appropriate)
  • Confined spaces and working at heights (certified)
  • Ice unthawing
  • Graffiti removal
  • Drain jetting and steaming, sizes up to 6"
  • Wet blasting (sand and water mix) for rust and paint removal
  • Food processing plant sanitizing
  • Concrete floor degreasing and resealing
  • Warehouse and garage cleaning
  • Steaming
  • Gas station canopies
  • Kitchen exhaust cleaning, compliant with NFPA 96

Industrial

For plants, yards, food facilities, and heavy-duty sites we align methods with your safety and environmental rules, including degreasing, sanitizing, wet blasting, drain work, and high-reach or confined-space programs where certified.

Fleet & equipment cleaning

Sals Mobile Truck Wash offers flexible hours and mobile units for trucks, trailers, and heavy equipment, with efficient, consistent washing when it fits your operation.

Commercial kitchens & exhaust

Kitchen exhaust and hood systems cleaned to align with NFPA 96 expectations, using methods appropriate for grease, access, and your schedule.

Agricultural services

  • Remove sludge and contaminants from heavy agricultural equipment
  • Tractors and plows: power washing
  • Sanitization of barns, stables, and cattle pens
  • Sanitize milking parlours
  • Remove faeces and food remains from livestock living quarters
  • Clean and sanitize greenhouses, warehouses, and other outbuildings
